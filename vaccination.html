Vaccination Information Center

Stay protected with up-to-date vaccination information and schedules

🍼

At Birth

BCG: Protects against Tuberculosis (TB).
Hepatitis B (1st dose): Prevents Hepatitis B (liver infection).
👶

6 Weeks – 6 Months

DPT (3 doses): Diphtheria, Pertussis (Whooping Cough), Tetanus.
Polio (3 doses): Prevents Polio (paralysis).
Hib (3 doses): Prevents Meningitis, Pneumonia.
Rotavirus (2 doses): Prevents severe Diarrhea.
PCV (3 doses): Pneumonia, Meningitis, Sepsis.
Hepatitis B (2nd & 3rd dose): Liver infection prevention.
👦

9–12 Months

MMR (1st dose): Measles, Mumps, Rubella.
Varicella (1st dose): Chickenpox.
Hepatitis A (1st dose): Liver infection prevention.
👧

16–24 Months

DPT, Polio Boosters
MMR (2nd dose)
Varicella (2nd dose)
🏫

5–6 Years

DPT Booster
Polio Booster
MMR (if missed earlier)
🧑

10–16 Years

Tdap Booster: Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis.
HPV (2 doses): Cervical & other cancers prevention.
Meningococcal Vaccine: Meningitis prevention.
🧓

Adults (≥18 Years)

Td Booster: Every 10 years (Tetanus, Diphtheria).
Influenza Vaccine: Annual flu prevention.
Hepatitis B: If not completed earlier.
👴

Older Adults (≥60 Years)

Pneumococcal Vaccine: Pneumonia, Meningitis prevention.
Shingles Vaccine: Prevents shingles and complications.
Influenza Vaccine: Annual flu prevention.
🤰

Pregnant Women

Tdap Vaccine: Protects mother and newborn (given in every pregnancy).
Influenza Vaccine: Prevents maternal & infant flu risk.

Important Vaccination Guidelines

  • Consult with healthcare providers before getting any vaccination
  • Keep vaccination records updated and easily accessible
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedules for optimal protection
  • Report any adverse reactions to healthcare providers immediately
  • Stay informed about vaccine updates and new recommendations
  • Consider vaccination needs before traveling internationally
Important Notice: Vaccination schedules may vary by region and individual health conditions. Consult a qualified healthcare provider for personalized advice.
